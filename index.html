About Us

At Ant Decorations, We Specialize In Styling Homes. Our Approach To Styling A Home Is A Proven Professional Process. “My experience in the interior design industry has exposed me to so many amazing ideas, brilliant people, as well as exciting and often exotic places. These events have influenced my sensibility and sense of adventure.”

Ant Decorations is an established company that has been delivering professional interior styling, design/decorating expertise for years. Antonio Baldeon, CEO of Ant Decorations obtained his BSc in Architecture from Technical University of Dortmund, Germany and had his MSc from the Woodbury University San Diego in Interior Architecture. He also obtained his license as an Interior Designer. Antonio seeks to create work that is comfortable and intelligent with the sheer practicality of excellence evident in all his work. Antonio has worked on a variety of projects, ranging from the exquisite Caesars Palace Suites to the Dubai Hills Golf Villa in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.
